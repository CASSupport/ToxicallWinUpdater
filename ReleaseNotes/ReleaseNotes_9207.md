Enhancements include moving the file attachments to the database, Route of Exposure by Substance, Audit Tracking enhancements, and more.


Poison Center feedback is critically important to ensure we can enhance Toxicall to meet the needs of all poison centers.

### Enhancements

\# File attachments are now stored in the database rather than in a file. This will help reduce the risk of orphaned files by maintaining them with the case data.
\# Route of Exposure can now be captured at the substance level. You can still capture the Route at the case level if desired. The case level will also show all routes based on each substance.
\# Several view enhancements, including how the case tab is presented with the substance description
\# Audit tracking has been further expanded with more changes to come in the next build
