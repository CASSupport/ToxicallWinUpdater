New support for non-integrated websites like drugs.com! Now perform drug imprint searches and bring data back into the toxiCALL substance. You can also capture additional details like concentration, formulation and even things like MSDS Number, CAS Number and more! This is supposed for all configured website lookups including Micromedex, Toxinz, and more!





Poison Center feedback is critically important to ensure we can enhance Toxicall to meet the needs of all poison centers.
